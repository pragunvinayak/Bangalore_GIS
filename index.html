Welcome

Road network and land use data is cumbersome to collect, even harder to screen and make it suitable for analysis. GIS Data finds application in a wide array of specializations like transportation engineering, urban planning, water resource management etc. Bangalore, though the hub for many research institutions, has very few sources of readily available open sourced GIS data for students - most data analytics companies charge exorbitant fees for such data.

Open Bangalore is a free repository with well-organized data relating to ward maps, lists of BMTC bus stops and routes, weather and census of 2000. But till the initiation of this depository limited GIS data was available for roads, bus stop locations and land use pattern in Bangalore.

This depository adds to the existing data by including .shp, *.shx, .dbf formats for the following :
1) Road Network - Well classified into Arterial, Primary, Secondary and Tertiary Road systems (all different files)
2) Land Use Maps with classifications as per BMP
3) BMTC B-Track Bus Stops with attributes like lat-long coordinates, name, bus routes catered
4) BBMP Election wards with Names, Ward Numbers, Population and Area
